Web Changes

This is where we'll announce the most recent additions to our web site. If you've visited us before and want to know what's changed, take a look here first.

July 2007 - Extreme In Security Inc. was modified into a general web hacking exercise/project available for those who purchase the William Stallings book, Computer Security: Principles and Practice.

Spring 2007 - For the fourth time in succession, students at the undergraduate and graduate levels continue to enjoy breaking into Extreme In Security Inc as part of their network security course project.

Fall 2005 - Extreme In Security was first constructed at Dakota State University for master's students in IS and IA, registered in the Network Security course by Dr. Sreekanth Malladi.
